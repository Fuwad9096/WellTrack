Mental Health Self-Assessments

These brief questionnaires are designed to help you reflect on your feelings and experiences. They are not diagnostic tools, but rather a way to gain initial insight into your mental well-being and guide you towards appropriate resources. Choose an assessment below to get started.

Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-item (GAD-7) Scale

The GAD-7 is a self-report questionnaire for screening and severity measuring of generalized anxiety disorder.

Take GAD-7 Assessment

Patient Health Questionnaire 9-item (PHQ-9) Scale

The PHQ-9 is a nine-item instrument that is used to screen for, diagnose, and monitor the severity of depression.
