Professional Help Resources

If you're facing significant challenges, please consider reaching out to mental health professionals. Here are some resources:

  • Campus Counseling Services: counseling@university.edu | (555) 123-4567

    Free and confidential support available to all enrolled students.

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

    Available 24/7 for crisis support in the U.S.

  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

    Free, 24/7, confidential text support.

  • The Jed Foundation: www.jedfoundation.org

    Protecting emotional health and preventing suicide for teens and young adults.
